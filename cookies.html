Perfect Cookie Recipe

  • 150g salted butter, softened
  • 80g light brown muscovado sugar
  • 80g granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 225g plain flour
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 200g plain chocolate chips or chunks
  1. Method 1

    2. Heat the oven to 190C/fan170C/gas 5 and line two baking sheets with non-stick baking paper.

  2. Method 2

    3. Put 150g softened salted butter, 80g light brown muscovado sugar and 80g granulated sugar into a bowl and beat until creamy.

  3. Method 3

    4. Beat in 2 tsp vanilla extract and 1 large egg.

  4. Method 4

    5. Sift 225g plain flour, ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda and ¼ tsp salt into the bowl and mix it in with a wooden spoon.

  5. Method 5

    6. Add 200g plain chocolate chips or chunks and stir well.

  6. Method 6

    7. Use a teaspoon to make small scoops of the mixture, spacing them well apart on the baking trays. This mixture should make about 30 cookies.

  7. Method 7

    8. Bake for 8 to 10 mins until they are light brown on the edges and still slightly soft in the centre if you press them.

  8. Method 8

    9. Leave on the tray for a couple of mins to set and then lift onto a cooling rack.
